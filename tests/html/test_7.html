Blind Assessment

Gently fruit-forward, cocoa-toned. Cocoa nib, raspberry, almond butter, pink grapefruit zest, cedar in aroma and cup. Delicately sweet structure with balanced, juicy acidity; crisp, satiny mouthfeel. Resonant finish that consolidates to cocoa-toned almond and citrus.

Notes

Produced at multiple farms owned by Carcafe, entirely of the Geisha variety of Arabica, and processed by the traditional washed method (fruit skin and flesh are removed from the beans before they are dried). RamsHead Coffee Roasters is a family-owned company founded by Bozeman, Montana natives Caleb and Laura Walker that specializes in direct trade microlot coffees. For more information, visit www.ramsheadcoffee.com .

Bottom Line

A delicate, confident Colombia Geisha cup with leading notes of fresh raspberry and rich almond with a cocoa nib throughline.
