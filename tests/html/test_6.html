Blind Assessment

Crisply cocoa-toned, nut-driven. Cocoa powder, hazelnut butter, fresh-cut fir, raisin, brown sugar in aroma and cup. Sweet structure with pert acidity; lightly satiny mouthfeel. The sweetly nutty finish is framed by notes of fresh-cut fir.

Notes

Certified USDA Organic and Fair Trade. Processed by the traditional washed method (fruit skin and pulp removed before drying). Rusty Dog Coffee is a Madison, Wisconsin-based specialty coffee roaster. Visit www.rustydogcoffee.com for more information.

Bottom Line

A straight-ahead Peru cup with leading notes of cocoa powder and rich hazelnut, sweet and deep.
